# SuperStore-Data-Visualisation-and-Storytelling
**Summary**
An interactive Tableau dashboard analysing £2.3M in sales data to evaluate revenue, profitability, regional performance, product-level contribution, and monthly trends. The dashboard highlights key business drivers and identifies opportunities to improve margin and regional growth.
**Sales by Region** - A bar chart indicating profit by region. West shows the largest number of sales, followed by East, Central, and South. There is a map depicting sales by region using tooltips
**Sales trend over time** - This chart shows seasonal demand in Q4.
Profit by sub-category shows which sub-category generates more profit; in this case, copiers generate the most profit and tables the least.
**Key Performance Indicators(KPIs)** include total sales, total profit, and profit margin(%).
Total Sales: "£2,297,201."
Total Profit: "£286,397."
Profit Margin: 12.47%

A final dashboard connecting all the analytics in an interactive way

**KEY INSIGHTS**
Regional dominance - The West region is the top contributor, generating over £108k in profit, significantly outperforming the South and Central regions.
Sub-category dominance - Copiers and Phones are the primary profit engines. Conversely, Tables are a significant outlier, currently operating at a loss.
Seasonality - Sales and profits show a clear Q4 peak, with a sharp increase in demand during November and December.

**Recommendations to maximize profits:**
Since there is a seasonality, the stock level should be increased in Q4. This focuses on inventory.
The tables sub-category must be investigated to reduce losses. This focuses on product mix.
